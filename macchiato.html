Traditional Macchiato

In the most basic terms, macchiato is an espresso with a dash of milk.

The name macchiato comes from Italian, and it means marked, stained, or spotted.

This description relates to the milk's mark when you add it to the dark, rich espresso.

The quantity of milk is just enough to lighten the color of the coffee and pierce the smooth, black surface.
