Affogato

The affogato is a dessert made with two main ingredients: vanilla ice cream and espresso.

Originally invented in Italy, the word "affogato" literally translates as "drowned" in English.

This is fitting, as the affogato is no more than a scoop of ice cream "drowned" in espresso.
